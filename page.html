Our journey

We envisioned a tool that turns sequences into 3D visualizations, explains folding and interactions, and adapts to each student's learning style.

What started as a personal passion project evolved into an interactive AI platform designed to make biology tangible, engaging, and personalized for every learner.

Over the last decade, our team has been responsible for some of the biggest research breakthroughs in AI, many of which underpin the flourishing molecular biology education we see today.

Our model

At the core of AION Bio is a smart AI tutor that studies how students interact with concepts.

Input: Students enter a protein, DNA, or RNA sequence.

Visualize: The AI generates a 3D structure, highlights active sites, and shows folding.

Personalize: It observes how students explore, identifies hesitation or mistakes, and provides adaptive explanations, analogies, and mini-quizzes.

Learn & Improve: Each interaction helps the AI give even better guidance the next time.

This ensures every student experiences hands-on, interactive learning tailored to their style, making molecular biology both fun and memorable.
